Experiment #0.3

Each driver maintains a constant turning rate and will try to maintain a constant speed but will brake to prevent a crash. If a car comes to a complete stop, the driver will shut off the car's engine. A driver uses one foot to drive and so cannot switch instantly from braking to accelerating or vice versa. Braking is four times as strong as acceleration.
